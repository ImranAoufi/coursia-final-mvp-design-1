Reflect on your learning by completing the following tasks: 
- Write out the Arabic alphabet from Alif to Ya, focusing on correct shapes. 
- Record yourself pronouncing the letters and listen for accuracy. 
- Create flashcards for the letters you find most challenging. 
- Practice writing and pronouncing at least five Arabic words using the letters learned. 
- Reflect on the sounds that were most difficult for you and create a plan to practice them.
