Welcome to the first video on crafting a scalable business model. In this video, we will explore what a scalable business model is and why it is crucial for growth. A scalable business model allows a company to increase revenue without a corresponding increase in costs. This means that as demand for your product or service grows, your business can expand without losing profitability. We will discuss various examples of scalable models, such as SaaS, franchises, and digital products. Additionally, we will cover the importance of technology and automation in creating a scalable model. By the end of this video, you will have a clear understanding of the characteristics that define a scalable business model and how to identify if your business has the potential to scale.
