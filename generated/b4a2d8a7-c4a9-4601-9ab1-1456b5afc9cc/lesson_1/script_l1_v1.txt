Welcome to 'Identifying Your Comfort Zones.' In this video, we will explore the areas in your life where you feel most at ease. First, take a moment to think about your daily routines. What activities do you do without hesitation? These are your comfort zones. Next, consider the emotions tied to these activities. Do they bring you joy, or do they keep you stagnant? Write down three areas where you feel comfortable. Now, let's identify the potential barriers that keep you from stepping outside these zones. Reflect on how these barriers have shaped your decisions. As we move forward, remember that recognizing your comfort zones is the first step to breaking free.
