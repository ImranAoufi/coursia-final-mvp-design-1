In this segment, we will discuss 'Stories of the Prophets: Lessons for Us.' The story of Prophet Yusuf teaches us resilience and trust in Allah’s plan. Reflect on a time when you faced adversity and how you can embody Yusuf's patience. The story of Prophet Ibrahim teaches us about unwavering faith. What are some beliefs you hold that require courage? Finally, we have the story of Prophet Muhammad, who exemplified compassion and forgiveness. Consider someone in your life you can forgive this week. Each of these stories provides valuable lessons that we can apply in our daily lives. Let’s strive to embody these teachings!
