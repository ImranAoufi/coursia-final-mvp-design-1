Reflect on your recitation practice. Consider the following tasks:
- Identify three letters that challenge your pronunciation and practice them.
- Write down the meaning of a verse you recited and reflect on its significance in your life.
- Set a daily intention for your recitation and note how it affects your connection to the Quran.
- Record yourself reciting and listen for areas of improvement.
- Share your reflections with a friend or family member to deepen your understanding.
