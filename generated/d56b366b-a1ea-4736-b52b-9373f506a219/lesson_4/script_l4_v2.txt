In this video, we’ll cover how to finalize your application for impact. First, review your entire application. Take a break and come back to it with fresh eyes. This will help you catch any mistakes or areas for improvement. Next, consider asking a trusted friend or mentor to review your application. They can offer valuable feedback and catch things you might have missed. Additionally, focus on your achievements. Highlight your most relevant experiences and skills that align with the position you’re applying for. Finally, ensure you’ve included all required documents and that they’re formatted correctly. Submit your application with confidence knowing you've put your best foot forward. Good luck!
