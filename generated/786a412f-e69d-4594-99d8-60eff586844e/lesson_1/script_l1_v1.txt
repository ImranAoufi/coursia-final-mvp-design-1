Welcome to 'Finding Your Niche: The Key to Success'. In this video, we will explore how to identify market gaps. Start by assessing your interests and skills. What do you love doing? Next, conduct market research. Look for areas where demand exceeds supply. Use tools like Google Trends or social media to find trending topics. Analyze your competitors. What are they missing? Finally, validate your niche idea by surveying potential customers or testing a minimum viable product. Remember, a successful niche combines your passion with market needs.
