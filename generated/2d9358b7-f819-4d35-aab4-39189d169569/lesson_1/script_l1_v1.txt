Welcome to the first part of our lesson on 'Understanding the YC Ecosystem'. In this video, we will explore what Y Combinator is and how it operates. Y Combinator, often abbreviated as YC, is a startup accelerator that provides funding, advice, and connections to early-stage startups. Founded in 2005, YC has helped launch over 2,000 companies, including famous names like Dropbox, Airbnb, and Reddit. The program runs twice a year, with selected startups receiving seed money, mentorship, and resources to help them grow. Participants present their ideas to a room full of investors during a Demo Day at the end of the program.
