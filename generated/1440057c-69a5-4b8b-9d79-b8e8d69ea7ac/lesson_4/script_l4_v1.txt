Welcome to 'Mastering Your Interview: Preparing for Tough Questions.' In this video, we'll cover how to effectively prepare for the toughest interview questions you might face. First, let's identify common tough questions such as 'Tell me about a time you failed' or 'What is your greatest weakness?' Next, we’ll discuss the STAR method: Situation, Task, Action, Result. This method helps you structure your answers clearly. Practice by writing down your own STAR responses for these questions. Remember, the key is honesty and focusing on what you learned from each experience. Finally, rehearse your answers aloud to build confidence.
